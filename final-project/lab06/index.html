Site Evaluation

Website Name: Maryland SPCA
URL: https://www.mdspca.org/
The Maryland SPCA aims to find people who want to adopt pets and encourage donations to support animals in need.

First Impression

Overall, the website is visually appealing and features numerous interactive elements. It offers information on who to contact, what resources are available, and where to get involved to support the cause. The events section highlights ways to engage and contribute to helping the animals and the organization. Additionally, the statistics displayed at the top of the page showcase the positive impact they have made in improving the lives of pets in need.


Results

After reviewing the website and analyzing the results from the Accessibility Checker, it was found that the Maryland SPCA site is not compliant with United States law, scoring only 61%. Several elements may hinder accessibility for individuals who are blind, have low vision, are deafblind, or experience mobility challenges and color blindness. These failing elements can interfere with how assistive technologies interpret and relay information to users.   2. Apply alternative text to buttons and images with links to be more accessible for those who rely on screen readers.
