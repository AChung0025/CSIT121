PNG

GIF stands for Portable Network Graphics
This image format supports millions of colors with the ability to have multiple layers and semi/transparent backgrounds. They are most used when making logos. PNG images are lossless compression files, no data is lost when the image is edited and saved. I use this PNG image of the Instagram logo since this is the app I use the most, and as shown, rescaling and exporting did not affect the quality.
